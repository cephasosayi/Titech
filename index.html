About Titech 2021

TITECH 2021 is the second largest annual Zonal Conference of Computing Students in the zone which is being followed by the National Conference. The theme for this convention is TITECH 2021 “TRANSFORMING INNOVATION FOR TECHNOLOGICAL SUSTAINABILITY” This is a 4-day event scheduled to hold from 14th – 17th September 2021 at Abuja. This event will host more than 1,200 (One thousand, two hundred) students from all twenty-three (23) higher institutions in the zone.
